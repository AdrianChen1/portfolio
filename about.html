Hello, I'm Adrian

I'm a sophomore in high school with a deep passion for storytelling, history, and creative expression. My work bridges the gap between past and present, exploring how revolutionary ideals continue to shape our modern world through photography, journalism, and multimedia projects.

As a photographer and writer, I'm drawn to stories that preserve cultural heritage and illuminate the human experience. My unique perspective is shaped by my active involvement in a Fife and Drum Corps, where I've learned firsthand about the power of tradition, discipline, and collective memory.

Through my portfolio, I aim to demonstrate not just technical skill, but a genuine commitment to meaningful storytelling. Whether capturing moments through the lens or crafting narratives with words, I strive to create work that inspires reflection and connection.

Fife and Drum Corps

My involvement with the Fife and Drum Corps has been transformative, teaching me about dedication, teamwork, and the importance of preserving historical traditions. Through countless hours of practice and performances, I've developed a profound appreciation for how music and ceremony can connect us to our past.

This experience directly informs my creative work. The discipline required in musical performance translates to my photography and writing, while the historical context enriches my understanding of the stories I tell. It's not just about playing period music‚Äîit's about embodying and sharing the spirit of revolutionary America.

Being part of this tradition has shown me the value of well-roundedness and the importance of contributing to something larger than myself. These lessons shape my approach to all my creative endeavors.
