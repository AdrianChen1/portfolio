PLACEHOLDER IMAGE INSTRUCTIONS

Until you add your own images, you can use these online placeholder services by updating the HTML img src attributes:

1. Simple color placeholders:
   https://placehold.co/800x600/1A1A1A/FFFFFF?text=Your+Text

2. Random photos:
   https://picsum.photos/800/600

3. Unsplash random:
   https://source.unsplash.com/800x600/?photography

Example replacements in HTML:
<img src="https://placehold.co/800x600/1A1A1A/FFFFFF?text=Photography" alt="Photo">

OR create simple placeholder files using this command:
# For Mac/Linux:
for dir in hero about articles photography projects; do
  cd $dir 2>/dev/null || continue
  for i in {1..3}; do
    convert -size 800x600 xc:#1A1A1A -pointsize 40 -fill white -gravity center -annotate +0+0 "Placeholder" placeholder-$i.jpg 2>/dev/null || echo "ImageMagick not installed"
  done
  cd ..
done
