My Approach to Journalism

Good journalism is more than reporting factsâ€”it's about understanding context, listening deeply, and telling stories that resonate with truth and humanity. I approach each piece with curiosity, respect for my subjects, and a commitment to thorough research.

Whether writing about historical traditions, cultural movements, or individual stories, I strive to create work that is both informative and engaging. My background in photography informs my writing, helping me to see and describe scenes with vivid detail.
